Victoria Island is one of Nigeria's busiest centres of banking and commerce with most major Nigerian and international corporations headquatered on the Island. It is also rich in stylish bars and clubs, as well as posh hotel lounges. Fone restaurants serve European cuisine or Nigerian staples while artisan bakeries, upmarket boutiques, and indie bookshops dot the streets. Some of our most popular locations include:

Civic Towers

Known as 'intelligent building', the Civic Tower, sits a state-of-the-art central communication tower which ensures efficient and seamless communication throughout the building

Photo Credit: Nupo Daniel [Unsplash]

Idumota Market

Idumota Market is a market located on Lagos Island. It is one of the oldest and arguably one of the largest markets in West Africa with thousands of lock-up shops occupying various buildings in the marke. The market is so popular that large sales are recorded as early as 7am. The market is made up of multiple storey-buildings with some measuring about 5 or more floors.

Photo Credit: Unavailable

Tarqwa Bay

Tarkwa Bay is an artificial sheltered beach located near the Lagos harbour in Nigeria. Due to its island status, it is only accessible by boat or water taxis.The beach, popular with swimmers and water-sports enthusiasts, also has a welcoming resident community.

Photo Credit: PixaHive.com

Lekki Conservation Center

The Lekki Conservation Centre is a plant and animal protection program that started as a joint initiative between the Nigerian Conservation Foundation and the now-defunct Lagos State Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperative. Lekki Conservation Centre sists on 190 acres of land bordered by the Lekki Lagoon. The program began as a way to protect Nigerian endangered species from going extinct

Photo Credit: Unavailable

Nike Art Gallery

Nike Art Gallery is owned ny Nike Davies-Okundaye. The gallery is one of the largest of it's kind in West Africs. Housed in five-storey Tall building in Lekki, it boasts a collection of 8,000 diverse artworks from various Nigerian artists like Chief Josephine Oboh Macleod

Photo Credit: Unsplash

Regus Landmark

Regus Nigeria delivers Office Spaces, Co-working, Virtual Offices, Businessworld & Much More. With flexible workspaces with 24/7 access fit for every budget, It's home to a good number of small business in the area.

Photo Credit: Unavailable

1004 Housing Estate

1004 Housing Estate is an 11-hectare housing estate in Victoria Island, Lagos. Originally named Federal Housing Estate, Lagos and designed by Isaac Fola-Alade, it was constructed in the 1970s as the biggest of its type at the time.The estate has 4 clusters of residential multistory condominiums; 6 high rise buildings and 4 low rise buildings comprising over 1000 apartments.

Photo Credit: Unavailable

Four Points by Sheraton

Four Points by Sheraton is a multinational hotel brand operated by Marriott International that targets business travelers and small conventions. As of June 30, 2020, Marriott operated 291 properties worldwide under the Four Points by Sheraton brand, with 53,054 rooms.
