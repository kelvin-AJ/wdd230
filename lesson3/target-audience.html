Target Audience:

The target audience is business owners and entrepreneurs situated in Victoria Island, Lagos Island and the Lekki Peninsula. It would inculde a wide range of businesses from small to middle range business owners seeking to improve visibility and credibily and also major businesses in the area willing to improve relationships and provide support to smaller businesses.

Alibert Furniture

Alibert furniture is known to be one of the largest family-owned furniture manufacturers in West Africa. Alibert furniture has two of its major showrooms in Victoria Island and Lekki Peninsula. Alibert furniture is hoping to increase her demographic to everyone possible because "everyone needs quality furniture".

Alibert furniture:
  • Can build connections with small, new businesses that would need to furnish work places.
  • Can recieve quality reviews from other members of the chamber.
  • is looking to get a list of active businesses in the area so the can adjust their advertisment strategies.

Arnegy

Arnegy Solar provides reliable and sustainable energy services for small , meduim and large businesses and residents across victoria island and the surrouding areas. While Arnegy is based in Victoria Island, Lagos, it hopes to provide sustainable solutions to energy reliability issues across emerging markets.

Arnegy:
  • Can host trainings with other member companies of the chamber to educate them on renewable energy and why it is needed for their businesses.
  • hopes to improve the company's name and build lasting relationships with neighbouring firms.
  • Can recieve quality reviews from other members of the chamber.
